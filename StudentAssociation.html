Details of First Year Engineering Students Association(FESA)




In charge Name of student association Logo Description
Sandeep S. Borgaonkar FESA First Year Engineering Students Association at PimpriChinchwad College of Engineering & Research is formed to introduce first year students to the qualities of leadership, teamwork, social awareness etc. The association is a students’ initiative made for students.

Functions
  • 1. To create a motivated team of students in the form of an association for their overall development.
  • 2. To make available a platform to students to develop their leadership and organizational skills.
  • 3. To provide an opportunity for students to interact with fellow students and display their extra curricular skills like art, photography, etc.
  • 4. To make students aware of their social responsibilities
